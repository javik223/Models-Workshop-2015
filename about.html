About
The Models Workshop

The Models Workshop Project is a training, empowerment, enlightenment and nurturing platform for aspiring models on the ethics and business of modeling.

The Models Workshop aims to broaden the scope and knowledge of the aspiring model, with the pre-requisite skills needed to be a top model. The Models Workshop is a platform for learning, practice, expression and networking.

The Models Workshop is a springboard for upcoming models to undergo coaching/mentoring classes with emphasis on experiential knowledge to put them in the right direction to succeed in their modeling careers.

The Workshop features prominent international speakers and industry leaders, spanning the photographic, top-modeling, brand and design, skin therapy, fashion, makeup and stylistic industries.

We are proud to have had respected speakers like: Eloho Aisien, Reze Bonna, Lola Maja, Joan Okorodudu, Uti Nwachukwu, Ovo Ogufere and Sola Oyebade.

We believe that Nigeria's modeling industry will continue to expand, and aim to give the next generation a head start on their careers.
